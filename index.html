A farm. A home. A rural hub for community.

For learning, growing, and exploring the natural world

Premium organic produce.

We sell our vegetables to households through a Community Shared Agriculture Program (CSA). Our vegetable boxes include carrots, garlic, onions, spinach, tomatores, broccoli, aspaaragus, seasonal salad greens, squash, and more!

Taste and stewardship.

Our food is flavorful and nutritious because our soils are alive and thriving. Our work as orgnanic farmers is one way we can steward our resources and take care of our planet. We work to influence climate change, soil erosion, and water conservation on a daily basis.

An access point for the community.

We love helping our local community create and maintain a meaningful connection to their food and to the land. Whether it be through farm tours, or recipe exchanges, we look forward to connecting with you!
